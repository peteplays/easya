We have a clear and simple vision; make a positive and lasting change on the world around us. Where did we start? By helping those who help others! We began by helping over worked and under paid physicians get into aesthetics!

easyAesthetics was founded by physicians and aesthetic consultants. As such, we have a deep and intimate understanding of the challenges physicians face owning and operating a medical practice. We also know the amount of cash-based revenue medical aesthetics can provide a practice, and the positive impact it can have on a physician's work/life balance. But the questions is, "will medical aesthetics work for my practice?" Unfortunately, simple demographics and surveys don't accurately depict whether or not aesthetics will work in a particular practice or business. easyAesthetics has come up with a novel concept - help practices and businesses realize whether or not aesthetics will work, before having to invest time, energy, and money!

We've created easyAesthetics with this goal in mind. easyAesthetics is the only aesthetic company that will help practices and businesses realize, through real world application, if aesthetics will be a good fit. We demonstrate how to perform the aesthetic procedures, how to market the aesthetic procedures, and how to structure an aesthetic business. easyAesthetics puts this into play, for each practice and business, before having to invest. This gives a unique glimpse into the logistical side of aesthetics, allowing businesses - medical or otherwise - to accurately assess how profitable aesthetics will be in their business, with their clients.

We at easyAesthetics know the challenges of getting into medical aesthetics very well. We saw how aesthetics had conventionally been implemented into practices and businesses, and knew there was a better way. A way to mitigate, if not completely remove, the risks businesses face when deciding to implement aesthetics. Changing the status quo is the challenge - easyAesthetics is the solution.

We said our vision is to make a positive and lasting change on the world around us. Helping physicians and business owners is only part of our story. A portion of every medical aesthetic treatment we perform goes to a deserving charity. This is what excites us most! If you are ready to give medical aesthetics a try, and you'd like to make a difference, contact us - we love to help!
